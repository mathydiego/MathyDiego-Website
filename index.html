--- title: Diego Avalos ---

I am not a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bristol, where I study logic and the philosophy of mathematics as a member of the Set Theoretic Pluralism project. I was previously a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Aberdeen, and before that I was a graduate student at the University of Bristol, where I received my PhD in 2015. The primary focus of my research at present is philosophical questions about reverse mathematics.

If you'd like to get in touch, please email me at benedict@eastaugh.net (PGP key).

  • The Set Theoretic Pluralism project has now moved to the University of Bristol, where I have taken up a position as a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Mathematics, working with Philip Welch.
